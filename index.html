Simply the Best Fluffy Pancakes

Step-by-Step to Perfect Pancakes

Click on Step 1 to get started

Mix Dry Ingredients in a Bowl

  • 2 cups All Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • 4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

Mix Wet Ingredients in a Separate Bowl

  • 1 3/4 cups Milk
  • 1/4 cup Butter
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Large Egg

Whisk the wet ingredients into the dry until smooth. Let batter rest while you heat up the pan.

Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan. Flip when the underside is golden and cook until golden.

Enjoy with warm maple syrup!
